Arunachal Pradesh Package

Arunachal Pradesh Adventure

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Arunachal Pradesh, known as the "Land of the Rising Sun." This package takes you through majestic mountain landscapes, serene monasteries, picturesque valleys, and vibrant tribal cultures, offering a perfect blend of adventure and cultural exploration.

Package Highlights:

  • Visit the famous Tawang Monastery, one of the largest monasteries in India.
  • Explore the scenic Sela Pass and its stunning high-altitude lake.
  • Discover the rich traditions and heritage of local tribal communities.
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the Eastern Himalayas.
  • Experience local cuisine and authentic cultural performances.

Package Includes:

  • Accommodation in comfortable hotels.
  • Daily breakfast and dinner.
  • Private transportation for sightseeing and transfers.
  • Guided tours to major attractions.
  • All necessary permits for travel within Arunachal Pradesh.

Starting Price: ₹18,999 per person

Best Time to Visit: October to April

Perfect For: Adventure seekers, nature lovers, photographers, and cultural explorers

Sikkim Package

Sikkim Himalayan Escape

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Discover the enchanting beauty of Sikkim, a Himalayan paradise known for its snow-capped mountains, peaceful monasteries, crystal-clear lakes, and vibrant culture. This package offers the perfect balance of relaxation, sightseeing, and adventure amidst some of India's most spectacular landscapes.

Package Highlights:

  • Explore the vibrant city of Gangtok and its popular attractions.
  • Visit the stunning Tsomgo Lake surrounded by majestic mountains.
  • Experience the beauty of Nathula Pass near the Indo-China border.
  • Discover ancient Buddhist monasteries and local traditions.
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the Himalayan mountain ranges.

Package Includes:

  • Accommodation in comfortable hotels.
  • Daily breakfast and dinner.
  • Private transportation for sightseeing and transfers.
  • Guided tours to major attractions.
  • Assistance with permits for restricted areas.

Starting Price: ₹17,999 per person

Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November

Perfect For: Nature enthusiasts, honeymooners, families, adventure seekers, and photography lovers.

Meghalaya Package

Meghalaya Nature Explorer

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Meghalaya, the "Abode of Clouds," renowned for its lush green hills, cascading waterfalls, crystal-clear rivers, and unique living root bridges. This package offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and cultural experiences.

Package Highlights:

  • Visit the famous Living Root Bridges of Cherrapunji.
  • Experience the crystal-clear waters of the Dawki River.
  • Explore the stunning Nohkalikai and Seven Sisters Waterfalls.
  • Discover the charming city of Shillong and its local attractions.
  • Enjoy scenic drives through picturesque valleys and rolling hills.

Package Includes:

  • Accommodation in comfortable hotels.
  • Daily breakfast and dinner.
  • Transportation for sightseeing and transfers.
  • Guided tours to major attractions.
  • Entry assistance for selected tourist sites.

Starting Price: ₹15,999 per person

Best Time to Visit: October to May

Perfect For: Nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, families, couples, and photography enthusiasts seeking a refreshing getaway amidst breathtaking natural beauty.
